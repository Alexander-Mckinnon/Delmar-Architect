Delmar Architects, P.A.

History of Firm

Delmar Architects, P.A. was founded by Eugene A. Delmar, FAIA, in 1959. He remains President of the company today. The firm was originally located in the attic of another architect in Silver Spring, MD. From there the firm moved to a new office on Sligo Avenue, in Silver Spring, designed by Eugene A. Delmar, FAIA, P.A. The firm was renamed Delmar Architects, P.A. when it moved to its present location in Olney, MD, in 1993.
