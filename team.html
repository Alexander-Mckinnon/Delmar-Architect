The Team

President

Mr. Eugene Delmar is founder and current President of Delmar Architects, P.A. He is a graduate of The Columbia University School of Architecture and holds a Masters Degree in Urban Design from the Catholic University of America. He served as an officer in the U.S. Army Corp. of Engineers in the Eastern Command Headquarters. Initially licensed to practice architecture in the State of Maryland, he has since obtained an NCARB Certificate and registration in the District of Columbia and Virginia. He has been a Fellow of the American Institute of Architects since 1974.

Vice-President

David A. Delmar began his architectural career in 1980 while attending college. He received a Bachelor of Science degree in architecture from the University of Maryland in 1985 and the professional degree of Bachelor of Architecture from the Catholic University of America in 1986. He became a registered architect in 1989. His experience with the firms of Eugene A. Delmar, FAIA, P.A. and Delmar Architects, P.A. includes design, production of contract documents, project management, coordination with civil, structural, mechanical, electrical and food service consultants, client communication, governmental reviews and CADD.

Architect & CADD Production

Daniel D. Weil, R.A. has been a practicing architect in the D.C. region since 1978. He graduated from the University of Toronto, from which he received a professional level degree in architecture. He has also attended the University of Michigan, taken classes in architecture, design and computers in Boulder, Colorado, Toronto, Ontario, and Frederick, Maryland. He was licensed as an architect in 1984 and more recently in Maryland. Since moving to the D.C. region he has worked for a number of firms, including VVKR, Inc.; Arthur Cotton Moore, Inc.; Mariani and Associates; Martin Reddy Architects, Suman Sorg and Associates; Hayes, Seay, Mattern and Mattern, Inc; and John S. Williams and Associates. He has been with Delmar Architects, P.A. since 1994.
